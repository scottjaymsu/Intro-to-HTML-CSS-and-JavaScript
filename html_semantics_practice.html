Unit Conversions
Temperature



Use this formula replacing the `C` with your temperature in Celsuis: (C × 9/5) + 32 to convert Celsius to Fahrenheit.
Weight



Use this formula replacing the `kg` with your weight in Kilograms: (kg × 2.205) to convert Kilograms to Pounds.
Distance



Use this formula replacing the `km` with your distance in Kilometers: (km ÷ 1.609) to convert Kilometers to Miles.
